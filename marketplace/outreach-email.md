## Notes for Sending

### Targeting Criteria
- GHL agencies with 10+ active sub-accounts
- Agencies posting in GHL Facebook groups (active users)
- Agencies offering marketing automation or CRM services
- Agencies that have mentioned AI, chatbots, or automation in their content

### Personalization Opportunities
- Reference their specific industry focus ("I saw you work with dental practices...")
- Mention their GHL community activity ("Saw your post in the GHL group about...")
- Reference their website/service offering ("You mention AI on your services page...")
- Note their sub-account count if visible ("Managing 20+ client accounts...")

### Sending Cadence
- **Day 0:** First touch email
- **Day 3:** Follow-up with social proof
- **Day 7:** Final, ultra-short follow-up
- **Day 14+:** If no response, add to nurture list (LinkedIn connection, GHL community engagement)

### What NOT to Do
- Don't spam GHL groups with the same message — post value, answer questions, mention Kyra when relevant
- Don't cold DM on Facebook — it's against group rules in most GHL communities
- Don't make revenue claims you can't back up — use the dental practice example as anecdotal, not guaranteed
- Don't position Kyra as replacing GHL features — it *extends* GHL, works with it
- Don't send more than 3 emails without a response — respect the silence
