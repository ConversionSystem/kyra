# Kyra AI Employee — Demo Video Script

**Duration:** 2:30  
**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover  
**Tone:** Confident, fast-paced, show-don't-tell  
**Goal:** Show a GHL agency owner that they can deploy an AI employee for a client in under 60 seconds — and that the AI actually works.

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] GHL sub-account with test contacts and a phone number configured
- [ ] Kyra dashboard logged in as agency account
- [ ] Clean browser (no distracting bookmarks/tabs)
- [ ] Screen recording tool ready (1920×1080, 30fps minimum)
- [ ] Test phone available to send/receive SMS
- [ ] Chrome with GHL and Kyra tabs open side by side

---

## SCENE 1: The Hook (0:00–0:15)

**Screen:** Black screen with text: *"Deploy an AI employee for your GHL client. 60 seconds."*

**Voiceover:**
> "Your GHL clients want AI. Not a chatbot — an AI employee that reads their CRM, responds to customers, and books appointments. Here's how fast you can deploy one."

**Visual:** Text fades. Cut to Kyra agency dashboard.

---

## SCENE 2: Connect GHL Sub-Account (0:15–0:40)

**Screen:** Kyra dashboard → Clients page → "Add Client" button

**Voiceover:**
> "From your agency dashboard, hit Add Client. Enter the business name — let's use 'Bright Smile Dental.' Pick the Dental industry template."

**Action on screen:**
1. Click **"Add Client"**
2. Type `Bright Smile Dental` in the name field
3. Select **"DentalAssist"** from the template dropdown
4. Click **"Connect GoHighLevel"**

**Voiceover:**
> "Now connect their GHL sub-account. Standard OAuth — pick the location, authorize, done."

**Action on screen:**
5. GHL authorization page appears
6. Select the sub-account location from dropdown
7. Click **"Authorize"**
8. Redirect back to Kyra dashboard — green checkmark appears next to GHL connection

**Voiceover:**
> "That's it. The AI employee is live. Let's test it."

**Timing note:** This entire sequence should feel fast. Cut any loading screens down to 1-2 seconds max.

---

## SCENE 3: Send a Test SMS (0:40–1:15)

**Screen:** Split view — phone (left), GHL conversations (right)

**Voiceover:**
> "I'm going to text this GHL number from my phone, like a real patient would."

**Action on screen:**
1. Show phone sending SMS: *"Hi, I need to schedule a cleaning. Do you have anything available this week?"*
2. Message appears in GHL Conversations panel (inbound)
3. Wait 15-30 seconds (show a subtle timer in the corner)
4. AI reply appears in GHL Conversations: a contextual, professional response checking availability and offering specific time slots

**Voiceover:**
> "Fifteen seconds. The AI read the conversation, checked context, and responded — through GHL, via SMS, to a real phone. No workflow. No decision tree. The AI figured out what to say."

**Action on screen:**
5. Show the SMS arriving on the phone
6. Highlight that the response is natural — not canned, not robotic

**Voiceover:**
> "Watch the quality of that response. It's not 'Thanks for reaching out, a team member will be with you shortly.' It actually addresses the request, asks the right follow-up question, and moves toward booking."

---

## SCENE 4: Show the AI's CRM Awareness (1:15–1:45)

**Screen:** Kyra dashboard → Client detail → Conversation log

**Voiceover:**

**Voiceover:**
> "The AI pulled the contact record from GHL before responding. It knows this is a returning patient. It sees their last appointment date. It's not starting from zero — it has context."

**Action on screen:**
4. Show the contact record in GHL with the AI's note added

## Post-Production Notes

- **Music:** Upbeat, modern, not distracting. Lower volume under voiceover. Something like Epidemic Sound's "tech/corporate" category but not cheesy.
- **Transitions:** Clean cuts, no fancy transitions. Speed is the message.
- **Cursor highlighting:** Use a subtle cursor highlight ring so viewers can follow clicks easily.
- **Loading states:** If any loading takes >2 seconds, speed up the footage. The demo should feel instant.
- **Captions:** Add subtitles — many GHL community members watch videos on mute in Facebook groups.
- **Thumbnail:** Split screen — phone with SMS on left, Kyra dashboard on right. Text: "AI Employee for GHL — 60 Second Deploy"
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 minimum. 4K preferred for marketplace listing.
- **Length:** Hard cap at 2:30. Cut aggressively. Every second should earn its place.

---

## Recording Order (Efficiency)

Record these in this order to minimize tab switching:

1. **Kyra dashboard shots** (Scenes 2, 4, 5) — all in one session
2. **Phone SMS shots** (Scene 3) — coordinate with a second camera or screen capture app
3. **GHL conversation view** (Scene 3) — capture after SMS is sent
4. **Intro and outro cards** (Scenes 1, 6) — create in Figma/Canva
5. **Voiceover** — record last, after editing footage, so timing matches perfectly
