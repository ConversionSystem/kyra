## FAQ Section Additions

**Q: What happens if Kyra gets acquired?**
A: Kyra is built on open-source OpenClaw technology. Your agent configurations, client data, and billing relationships are all exportable. Even in the unlikely event of an acquisition, your data and configurations remain yours. We've architected Kyra so agencies are never locked in.

**Q: Does Kyra share my clients' data with AI providers?**
A: No. Conversations are processed through our AI bridge (hosted in Frankfurt, EU) and are not used for training by any AI provider. We use Anthropic's commercial API, which has explicit no-training guarantees. Your client conversations are YOUR business data.

**Q: Can I switch AI models if one provider changes terms?**
A: Yes. Kyra supports multiple AI providers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini). If one provider changes pricing or terms, switch to another with one click. We're also building local model support for agencies that want zero cloud dependency.

**Q: What if I outgrow Kyra?**
A: We're built on open-source technology (OpenClaw). You can export your configurations, client data, and conversation history at any time. We believe the best way to earn your loyalty is to never force it.
