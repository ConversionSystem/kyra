## Longer Description
### The Problem
Agencies want to offer AI to clients but face three blockers:
1. **No infrastructure** — setting up AI agents per client is complex
2. **No white-label** — existing tools show the vendor's brand, not yours
3. **No scalability** — managing 10+ clients' AI from one place doesn't exist

### The Solution
Kyra lets agencies deploy, manage, and monetize AI workers for their clients — without writing code or managing infrastructure.

**For each client, you get:**
- A dedicated AI worker with its own personality, knowledge, and tools
- Connected to their channels (SMS, email, web chat, Telegram, WhatsApp)
- White-labeled under your brand
- Managed from one agency dashboard

**What makes Kyra different:**
- **50+ industry templates** — dental, real estate, cannabis, legal, and more. One click to configure.
- **Built on OpenClaw** — real autonomous agents, not chatbots. They use tools, remember context, and take actions.
- **Skills marketplace** — install community skills from ClawHub to extend capabilities
- **Two-sided template marketplace** — create templates, share them, earn credits when others install
- **Premium AI workers** — like our Vet SEO Worker that autonomously manages local SEO across 7 platforms

### Pricing
- **Lite** $99/mo — 3 clients, 500 credits
- **Pro** $249/mo — 10 clients, 1,500 credits
- **Scale** $499/mo — 30 clients, 2,500 credits
- 7-day free trial, no credit card required
