# 60-Second Demo Video Script

## Format
- Screen recording with voiceover
- Record at 1920x1080
- Use kyra.conversionsystem.com (production)
- Background music: subtle, upbeat (optional)

## Script (60 seconds)

### 0-5s — Hook
**[Show: Kyra landing page]**
"What if you could deploy an AI employee for every client — in under 60 seconds?"

### 5-15s — The Problem
**[Show: Agency dashboard, empty]**
"Agencies want to offer AI, but setting up agents per client is a nightmare. Infrastructure, configuration, training — it takes weeks per client."

### 15-25s — Sign Up + First Client
**[Show: Click "Add Client" → fill name + industry → click Create]**
"With Kyra, you add a client, pick their industry, and their AI worker is live. That's it."

### 25-35s — Template Store
**[Show: Templates tab → scroll through 50 templates → click one → Apply]**
"50 industry templates. Dental, real estate, cannabis, legal — each one pre-configured with the right personality, tools, and knowledge."

### 35-45s — Skills + ClawHub
**[Show: Skills tab → toggle on a few → switch to ClawHub marketplace → install one]**
"Every worker comes with 12 built-in skills. Need more? Install from ClawHub — thousands of community skills, one click."

### 45-52s — White Label
**[Show: Client portal with custom branding → web chat widget]**
"Everything is white-labeled. Your brand, your portal, your AI workers."

### 52-60s — CTA
**[Show: Pricing page or solo signup]**
"Kyra. Deploy AI workers for your clients. Try it free — no credit card needed."
**[Show: kyra.conversionsystem.com]**

## Recording Tips
- Use a clean browser (no bookmarks bar, no extensions showing)
- Log in as an agency with a few clients already set up
- Have at least 2-3 clients visible in the dashboard
- Move the cursor smoothly, don't rush
- Record voiceover separately for clean audio
- Total runtime: aim for 55-65 seconds
