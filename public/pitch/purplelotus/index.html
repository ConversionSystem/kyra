💜 After Delivery

The relationship doesn't end at the door

Currently: "Your order has been delivered!" then silence forever. With Kyra: 21 days of relationship building.

6

24-Hour Follow-Up

"How was everything? If anything wasn't perfect, reply here and we'll make it right." Catches problems before they become 1-star reviews.

7

Gated Review Request (72 hrs)

Only asks customers who rated 4 or 5 for a public review. Unhappy ones were already caught by the follow-up. This is how you go from 4.8 → 4.9.

8

Visit 2 Retention Nudge (7-21 days)

Only sent if no repeat order. "Ready for another Lotus Now run? Your next order gets $10 off." Visit 2 is the most critical retention inflection.

Why Kyra, not Zapier: Zapier can schedule a delayed message. Zapier cannot gate it based on a reply to a previous message. Kyra has memory — it knows what the customer rated and decides whether to ask for a review.
