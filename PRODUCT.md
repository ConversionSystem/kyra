# Kyra — Product Definition

## Official One-Liner
Kyra is a white-label AI workforce platform built on OpenClaw for agencies. Agencies use it to deploy, manage, and monetize autonomous AI workers for their clients — without writing code, without managing infrastructure.

## Who It's For
**Primary:** Digital agencies and GHL resellers
**Secondary:** Solo business owners (Kyra Solo Free)
