# Kyra Social Media Content Strategy

## Brand Voice

Bold. Confident. Results-focused. No fluff. Real numbers. Short punchy sentences. Steve Jobs energy.

We don't beg for attention — we earn it with undeniable results.

**Tone keywords:** Direct, authoritative, conversational, data-driven, aspirational
**Never:** Desperate, salesy, corporate-speak, vague promises

---

## Content Pillars

Every piece of content falls into one of four pillars. We rotate through them to keep the feed balanced and engaging.

### Pillar 1: Proof (40%)
Show the AI working. Real SMS screenshots. Real booking confirmations. Real revenue math. This is our strongest content — let the results speak.

### Pillar 2: Education (25%)
Teach agencies how AI workers fit into their business model. Pricing strategies, GHL setup guides, workflow tutorials. Position Kyra as the authority.

### Pillar 3: Transformation (20%)
Before/after stories. The agency owner who stopped texting leads at 2 AM. The operator who added $5K/mo without hiring. Emotional, relatable, aspirational.

### Pillar 4: Vision (15%)
Thought leadership on the future of agencies. Why AI workers are the next revenue line. Industry trends. Bold predictions. This builds the brand long-term.

## Platform Breakdown

### TikTok
- **Content type:** 15-60 second vertical videos, raw/authentic feel
- **Posting cadence:** 5-7x per week
- **Best times to post:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Phone-recorded, talking head, screen recordings of AI convos, green screen with screenshots
- **Goal:** Awareness + virality

### Instagram Reels
- **Content type:** 15-60 second vertical videos, slightly more polished than TikTok
- **Posting cadence:** 5-7x per week (repurpose TikTok content)
- **Best times to post:** 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Same as TikTok but remove TikTok watermark; also post to Stories
- **Goal:** Awareness + profile visits + link in bio clicks

### YouTube Shorts
- **Content type:** 15-60 second vertical videos
- **Posting cadence:** 3-5x per week (repurpose best TikTok/Reels)
- **Best times to post:** 2-4 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Repurpose top-performing short-form content
- **Goal:** Channel growth + funnel to long-form YouTube

### YouTube (Long-form)
- **Content type:** 5-15 minute tutorials, case studies, comparisons
- **Posting cadence:** 1-2x per week
- **Best times to post:** Thursday/Friday 2-4 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Screen share tutorials, talking head, high production value
- **Goal:** SEO + deep education + trust building

### Facebook
- **Content type:** Long-form text posts, polls, video reposts, group engagement
- **Posting cadence:** 1-2x per day
- **Best times to post:** 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Personal, story-driven, longer text. Engage in GHL-related groups.
- **Goal:** Community building + group engagement + retargeting audiences

### LinkedIn
- **Content type:** Text posts, carousels, thought leadership, data insights
- **Posting cadence:** 1x per day, 5 days per week
- **Best times to post:** 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM (EST) — Tuesday through Thursday peak
- **Style:** Professional but not boring. Lead with insight. Short paragraphs. Line breaks.
- **Goal:** B2B credibility + agency owner network + inbound leads

### Twitter/X
- **Content type:** Single tweets, threads, quote tweets, reply engagement
- **Posting cadence:** 3-5x per day (mix of original + replies)
- **Best times to post:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM (EST)
- **Style:** Punchy, opinionated, data-driven. Threads for depth.
- **Goal:** Brand awareness + authority + engagement

## Hook Formula

Every video/post opens with a scroll-stopper. Use one of these formulas:

### Video Hooks (First 3 Seconds)
1. **The Proof Hook:** "This AI worker just booked 3 appointments while I was eating lunch."
2. **The Contrast Hook:** "Stop doing [old way]. Here's what smart agencies do instead."
3. **The Math Hook:** "[$X/mo] × [Y clients] = [$Z/mo]. Here's how."
4. **The Question Hook:** "Why are agency owners still texting leads manually in 2026?"
5. **The POV Hook:** "POV: Your AI worker just closed a deal at 3 AM."
6. **The Callout Hook:** "If you're using GHL without AI workers, you're leaving money on the table."
7. **The Story Hook:** "Last week, one of our agencies added $2,000/mo with a single change."

### Text Post Hooks (First Line)
1. **Bold statement:** "The agency model is broken. Here's how to fix it."
2. **Counterintuitive:** "The best-performing agencies are firing their lead responders."
3. **Data lead:** "We analyzed 1,000 SMS conversations. Here's what the AI learned."
4. **Personal story:** "I used to respond to leads until 2 AM. Then I deployed an AI worker."
5. **Question:** "What would an extra $5K/mo do for your agency?"

### Rules for Hooks
- Never start with "Hey guys" or "In this video..."
- Lead with the result, not the process
- Create an open loop — make them need the answer
- Use specific numbers over vague claims
- Match the hook to the platform (TikTok = bold, LinkedIn = insightful)

## Content Repurposing Flow

```
YouTube Long-form → Cut into 3-5 Shorts/Reels/TikToks
     ↓
TikTok → Repurpose to Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts
     ↓
Best-performing Reels → Write as LinkedIn carousel + Twitter thread
     ↓
LinkedIn post → Expand into Facebook long-form post
     ↓
Twitter thread → Turn into email newsletter content
```

One idea. Seven platforms. Maximum reach with minimum effort.
